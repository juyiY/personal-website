About Me

I am a graduate student at UCLA majoring in Applied Statistics and Data Science. Having arrived in the United States at the age of 14, I have been greatly influenced by both Eastern and Western cultures. I spent two years in Kansas for high school and two years in the Bay Area for community college, where I was elected as the youngest and first Chinese student body president in college history at age 17.

After completing my undergraduate studies in Economics and working for a year, I decided to return to school and enhance my technical background. My post-graduation aspirations involve pursuing roles in Data Science and Product Management.
